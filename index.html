The thesis

Most crypto apps show you the price.
We show you the probability.

Charts tell you what already happened. Probability distributions — sourced live from Kalshi prediction markets — tell you what the smart money actually believes will happen. We render that belief as a smooth curve, then let you bet your gut against it.

How it works

Three taps. One call.

1

See the curve

Live probability distributions for six cryptos — year-end for BTC/ETH/SOL, monthly highs/lows for XRP/DOGE/BNB — aggregated from Kalshi market brackets. The taller the curve, the stronger the market's belief at that price.

2

Make your call

Drag the price tape to set any target. The app snaps to the matching bracket, surfaces the market's probability, and locks in your above-or-below prediction.

3

Track the market

Each call is continuously scored against the live consensus and current spot. Push alerts when brackets shift. A 90-day consensus-vs-spot history shows how sharp the market actually is.
