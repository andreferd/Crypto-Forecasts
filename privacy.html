Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 April 2026

Crowd Price does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on remote servers. The app has no backend.

Local-only data

All data stored by the app is local to your device only:

  • Your saved price predictions (symbol, price, direction, timestamp)
  • Your day-streak and accuracy log
  • Alert baselines for shift detection
  • Your onboarding preference
  • Optional Solana wallet public key, if you connect one — never transmitted

Outbound network requests

The app makes outbound network requests only to two public APIs: Kalshi (api.elections.kalshi.com) for prediction-market data, and CoinGecko (api.coingecko.com) for spot prices. These requests do not include any user identifier.

No third-party SDKs

The app uses no third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.

Wiping local data

You can wipe all local data via Settings → Clear local data inside the app.
